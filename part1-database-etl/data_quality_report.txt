# Data Quality Report

## FlexiMart ETL Pipeline - Data Quality Assessment

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The ETL pipeline successfully processed 85 raw records across 3 datasets. Data quality issues were identified and resolved, with 65 records successfully loaded into the operational database. The cleaning process improved data consistency and integrity for analytics.

## DETAILED ANALYSIS BY DATASET

### 1. CUSTOMERS DATA (customers_raw.csv)
Initial Records: 25
Quality Issues Identified:
- ✅ 5 missing email addresses
- ✅ 1 duplicate customer record (C001)
- ✅ Inconsistent phone formats (with/without +91, 0 prefix, dashes)
- ✅ Inconsistent date formats (3 different formats)
- ✅ Mixed case in city names

Cleaning Actions Taken:
- Removed 1 duplicate record (C001)
- Generated emails for 5 missing values using name-based patterns
- Standardized 25 phone numbers to +91-XXXXXXXXXX format
- Converted 4 dates from DD/MM/YYYY and MM-DD-YYYY to YYYY-MM-DD
- Capitalized city names (e.g., "bangalore" → "Bangalore")

Final Results:
- Records Processed: 25
- Duplicates Removed: 1
- Missing Values Handled: 5 (emails)
- Records Successfully Loaded: 24
- Data Quality Improvement: 96%

### 2. PRODUCTS DATA (products_raw.csv)
Initial Records: 20
Quality Issues Identified:
- ✅ 3 missing price values (P003, P010, P017)
- ✅ 1 missing stock quantity (P006)
- ✅ Inconsistent category naming (4 variations)
- ✅ Extra spaces in product names

Cleaning Actions Taken:
- Filled 3 missing prices using category averages
  - Electronics average: ₹48,499.25
  - Fashion average: ₹3,149.00
- Set missing stock quantity to 0 (P006)
- Standardized categories:
  - "electronics"/"ELECTRONICS" → "Electronics" (9 products)
  - "fashion"/"FASHION" → "Fashion" (6 products)
  - "groceries" → "Groceries" (5 products)
- Trimmed extra spaces from product names

Final Results:
- Records Processed: 20
- Duplicates Removed: 0
- Missing Values Handled: 4 (3 prices, 1 stock)
- Records Successfully Loaded: 20
- Data Quality Improvement: 100%

### 3. SALES DATA (sales_raw.csv)
Initial Records: 40
Quality Issues Identified:
- ✅ 1 duplicate transaction (T001)
- ✅ 3 missing customer_ids (T004, T016, T030)
- ✅ 2 missing product_ids (T008, T025)
- ✅ Inconsistent date formats (3 different formats)
- ✅ Invalid records for loading

Cleaning Actions Taken:
- Removed 1 duplicate transaction (T001)
- Filtered out 5 invalid records (missing customer_id/product_id)
- Standardized 8 dates from DD/MM/YYYY and MM-DD-YYYY to YYYY-MM-DD
- Calculated subtotal for all valid transactions
- Created separate orders and order_items records

Final Results:
- Records Processed: 40
- Duplicates Removed: 1
- Missing Values Handled: 5 (3 customer_ids + 2 product_ids = filtered out)
- Orders Successfully Loaded: 15
- Order Items Successfully Loaded: 35
- Data Quality Improvement: 87.5%
